Best Cities in Europe for Coffee Lovers

07/Sep/2023

Europe is home to some of the world's most renowned coffee cultures, with each country boasting its unique brewing traditions and specialty drinks. From the aromatic espresso of Italy to the cozy coffeehouses of Vienna, Europe offers a caffeinated journey like no other. If you're a coffee enthusiast, here are some of the best cities in Europe that should be on your radar:

Milan, Italy

Known as the fashion capital of Italy, Milan is also home to some of the country's finest espresso bars and cafes. From traditional coffee shops serving up rich and smooth brews to modern hangouts with sleek designs and trendy vibes, there's something for every taste in this bustling city. Be sure to try a classic Milanese cortado or a shot of espresso at one of the many historic cafes like Bar Basso or Caffe San Fedele.

Vienna, Austria

Vienna is often referred to as the coffee capital of the world. The city's coffeehouses, known as "kaffeehäuser," have been a cultural institution for centuries. These elegant establishments offer a wide range of coffee options, including the famous Viennese coffee. Try a Melange, a creamy coffee with frothy milk, and pair it with a slice of Sachertorte, a delectable chocolate cake.

Barcelona, Spain

This vibrant city is home to some of the best cafes in Europe with modern designs and lively atmospheres. Visit Carrer de Verge Mongat Street where you can find a variety of cozy coffee shops serving up expertly crafted espresso drinks like cortados, flat whites, or pour-over coffees. Be sure to try the famous Catalan Coffee (Café Bombón) which combines rich espresso with sweetened condensed milk and a thick layer of foam on top for an indulgent treat that's unique to Barcelona.

Berlin, Germany

As one of Europe’s most hipster cities, it comes as no surprise that Berlin has become a hub for coffee culture in recent years. From trendy independent cafes like Bulo Coffee Roasters or Kaffeebar to cozy classic establishments like Cafe Einstein or Schultheiss Brauerei, there’s something here for every type of palate and preference. Be sure not to miss out on a traditional Berlin-style coffee known as Flammkuchen which combines espresso with whipped cream foam to create an intense but smooth drinking experience that'll keep you going all day long!

Paris, France

The City of Light is also home to some truly exceptional cafes where one can savor world-class coffee in the heart of Europe’s most romantic city. Visit Café Procope for an authentic 17th century experience or stop by Le Comptoir du Relais, which has been serving top–notch brews since before World War II – both are sure to please even your highest standards! And don't forget about Les Deux Magots where famous writers like Hemingway used to hang out while sipping on their favorite coffee concoctions.

In conclusion, these five cities offer an unparalleled experience for any serious coffee lover visiting Europe – from traditional espresso bars to modern cafes with trendy vibes and expertly crafted drinks guaranteed to leave your taste buds wanting more! So, pack up those bags (or grab a cup of joe), travel through these beautiful cities, indulge in their rich coffee cultures & enjoy every moment along the way – bon voyage et buon caffè!
