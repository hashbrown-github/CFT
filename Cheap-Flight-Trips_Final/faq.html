Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Asked Before Booking

Booking a flight with Cheap Flight Trips is easy. Simply visit our website or use our mobile app, enter your travel details (departure and arrival cities, dates, and passenger information), and browse through available flight options.
To search for flights, simply enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and the number of passengers on our website. Click the "Search" button, and we'll display available flight options for you.
An e–ticket (electronic ticket) is a paperless electronic document with a unique confirmation number that neatly replaces the hassles of a paper ticket. When you purchase an e–ticket, we email it to you after the booking is complete. Simply print it out and bring it with you – along with a valid photo ID – to the airline counter when checking in for your flight.
You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" section on our website or email us on contact@cheapflighttrips.com. We are here to assist you with any pre-booking inquiries.

Questions Asked While Booking

Cheap Flight Trips provides various filters and sorting options to help you find the best flight for your preferences, including price, flight duration, layovers, and airline. You can compare and choose the one that suits you best.
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and in some cases, alternative payment options like PayPal. The accepted payment methods will be displayed during the booking process.
Depending on the airline and fare type, you may have the option to add baggage or other services during the booking process. These options will be presented to you before payment.
The necessary details of your terminal are available in your confirmation e-mail. It also includes details regarding the air carrier, seat number and departure time. And if you find an issue, please reach out to us with the reference number of your booking.
Yes, Cheap Flight Trips takes the security of your data seriously. We use encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your information during the booking process.

Questions Asked After Booking

Once you've completed your booking, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details of your flight. You can also access your booking information by logging into your Cheap Flight Trips account.
To make changes to your booking, you may need to contact the airline directly. Some changes can be made online through the airline's website, while others may require you to call their customer service.
If you require assistance or have questions about your booking, you can contact our customer support team through the "Contact Us" section on our website or email us on contact@cheapflighttrips.com. We're here to help you throughout your journey.

The refund and cancellation policies vary depending on the airline and fare type. You can find specific details in the terms and conditions displayed during the booking process. Please review them carefully before confirming your reservation.

Please note that specific policies and procedures may vary depending on the airline and the terms of your ticket, so it's essential to review the details provided during the booking process and contact the airline for any post-booking inquiries.
